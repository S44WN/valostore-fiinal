Frequently Asked Questions

What is my Riot ID?
Your Riot ID is a unique username that you use to login, like AryanBa3a, and not your display name, like Ba3a#5807.
Why do you need my password?
We need your password in order to get your store data from Riot Games. Please note that sharing just your password does not endanger your account. Learn more here .
Can I check my store without entering password?
Yes, we have designed our system such that you can still use our services without sharing your password with us. Learn more about Passwordless login here.
Where do I get help?
You can tweet us at Ba3a_Gamzo or S44WN
When does the Valorant store reset?
The in-game Valorant store resets every 24 hours at 1:00 am UTC / 8:00 pm EST / 6:30 am IST. The store is randomly generated for each Valorant player.
Can I purchase weapon skins from this website?
No, you can not purchase weapon skins direcly from this website. However, we show everything else about the skins that you get in store, like cost (VP) and finisher video.
Are battlepass skins included in the store rotation?
No, battlepass skins are never included in the store rotation. In order to get those you have to purchase the battle-pass and then... if you are rich enough - buy all at once or you got to grind the game to finish the battle-pass and get all the good stuff.
Are agent contract skins included in the rotation?
No, agent contract skins are exclusive and are not included in the daily store rotation. You have to complete the agents' contract to get those.
